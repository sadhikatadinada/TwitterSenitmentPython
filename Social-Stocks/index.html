Social Stocks

Inspired by the GameStop incident; Social Stocks is an online platform which predicts stock prices based on the social media and market trends to prevent unwanted and sudden market crashes. This project is designed to graph the sentiment analysis of up to 1000 tweets of a certain stock. Using this, the user can see which stocks are trending positively, or negatively, and watch out for those stocks. This is a way for people to expand their understanding of the stock market by seeing what other people think about a certain stock.
